“A Social Network for Evacuees is a digital platform designed to bridge the gap between displaced individuals and essential resources. The application helps evacuees find safe places, connect with others in the same disaster area, and request help from governments or organizations. Featuring real-time mapping, community support forums, and SOS tools, this network ensures no one is left behind, even in the most challenging times.”
